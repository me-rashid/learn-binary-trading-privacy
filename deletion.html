What Data Gets Deleted?

The following data will be permanently deleted:

  • Your account information (email, name, profile)
  • All learning progress and bookmarks
  • Community posts and comments
  • Support ticket history
  • Reading history and preferences
  • Push notification tokens
  • All associated metadata and logs

What may be retained:

  • Anonymized usage statistics (not linked to you personally)
  • Legal compliance records (if required by law)
  • Backup data for up to 30 days (automatically deleted)

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does deletion take?

We process requests within 7 business days. You'll receive email confirmation when complete.

Can I recover my data after deletion?

No. Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered. Make sure you have exported any important information first.

What if I want to use the app again?

You'll need to create a new account. All previous data will be gone.

Is my data really deleted or just hidden?

Your personal data is permanently deleted from our active databases. Backup copies are automatically deleted within 30 days.
